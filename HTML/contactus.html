Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change or cancel my flight?

You can change or cancel your flight by logging into your SkyJourney account and accessing your bookings. Alternatively, you can contact our customer support team for assistance. Please note that change and cancellation fees may apply depending on your fare type and how close you are to your departure date.

What is the baggage allowance for my flight?

Baggage allowances vary depending on your fare class, destination, and frequent flyer status. Generally, economy class passengers are allowed one carry-on bag (max 7kg) and one personal item. Checked baggage allowances typically range from 20kg to 32kg. You can find specific details for your flight in your booking confirmation or by visiting our baggage policy page.

How do I select my seat?

You can select your seat during the booking process or after booking by managing your reservation online. Some seats may require an additional fee, while others are available for free selection depending on your fare class. Seat selection typically opens 24-48 hours before departure if you haven't selected one earlier.

What happens if my flight is delayed or canceled?

In case of delays or cancellations, we will notify you via email or SMS using the contact information provided during booking. Depending on the circumstances, we may offer alternative flights, refunds, or compensation as per our terms and conditions and applicable regulations. You can check your flight status in real-time on our website or mobile app.

How do I request special assistance?

You can request special assistance (wheelchair service, medical needs, etc.) during the booking process or by contacting our customer support team at least 48 hours before your flight. We recommend arriving at the airport earlier than usual to allow sufficient time for assistance procedures.
